## Decisions made
- **Pivoted from "Do Less" to Aging Parent space** — team has personal pain here, market is $7B→$50B, massively underfunded ($22.5M total startup funding)
- **Key insight locked: build for the CHILD, not the parent** — every existing player (Emoha, Anvayaa, Nila, Praan Health, Samarth) solves for the parent's health/safety. Nobody solves the child's chaos.
- **Financial/coordination gap identified as widest open space** — health/care side getting crowded (5+ funded players). Financial visibility, sibling coordination, document management = empty field.

## What broke / surprised us
- Session 1 killed aging parents ("distribution mismatch") but team's personal connection pulled it back. Sometimes distribution follows conviction, not the other way around.
- Praan Health (praan.health) — missed in initial research. Founded 2024, backed by Rainmatter (Nithin Kamath), 5x revenue growth. Reinforces thesis: health side is crowded, financial side is open.
- GenWise failed December 2025 with 3M users and $0.04 ARPU. Root cause: sold TO seniors, not FOR seniors. The buyer MUST be the adult child.
- AI costs dropped 300x in 3 years — makes AI-powered coordination feasible at Rs 500-3,000/month price point.
