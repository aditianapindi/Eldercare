# Idea: "Unblock Your Voice" — LinkedIn Story Unblocker

## Thesis

99% of LinkedIn users never post. Not because they have nothing to say — because they can't see their own story as worth sharing. This tool reframes your lived experience into a story strangers care about.

---

## The Problem

People have real stories — career breaks, pivots, failures, lessons — but don't share them because:
- **Cringe factor** — "this is too personal"
- **Vulnerability fear** — "what will people think?"
- **Comparison trap** — "my story isn't polished enough"
- **Can't see the angle** — "why would anyone care about this?"

What exists today doesn't solve this:
- ChatGPT/Gemini can write a post FOR you — but it sounds fake, not like you
- LinkedIn coaches teach frameworks — but frameworks don't fix the fear
- Reddit roasting — slow, inconsistent, not actionable

**The gap:** Nobody helps you see why your specific story matters to OTHER people. The reframe is the product, not the writing.

---

## The Product

### Screen 1 — The prompt

One text box. One question:

> "What's something you've experienced that you think about sharing but never do?"

Examples shown as placeholders:
- "I took a 5-year career break when everyone said it was career suicide"
- "I switched from engineering to PM and still feel like a fraud"
- "I got fired and it was the best thing that happened to me"

### Screen 2 — The output (3-4 cards)

**Card 1: "Why people will care"**
The universal angle in your story. Not what happened to you — why it resonates with thousands of others.

> "You chose yourself over the script. Most people fantasize about this but never do it. That's why this story hits — it's aspirational, not sad."

**Card 2: "Your hook" (first 2 lines)**
The scroll-stopping opener. Written sharp, not corporate.

> "Everyone told me a career break would end my career. Five years later, here's what actually happened."

**Card 3: "The roast" (calls out your hesitation)**
Humor that makes fun of your excuses, not you. The nudge to actually post.

> "You've been sitting on this story for how long? Meanwhile someone just posted a photo of their laptop at a coffee shop captioned 'grinding' and got 300 likes. Post your actual story."

**Card 4: "Ready to go" (full draft)**
A short, punchy version of the post. Sounds like a real person, not AI-polished. Written in their voice based on how they described the story.

**Bottom of page:**
- Big "Copy to clipboard" button
- "Too scared? Send this to a friend first" (share link)
- "Post it. The bar is lower than you think."

---

## Why This Isn't "Just Use ChatGPT"

1. **It reframes, not writes.** The value is showing you WHY your story matters, not ghostwriting a post.
2. **The roast/humor is the differentiator.** Every AI writing tool is earnest and polished. This one calls you out with love.
3. **The output is designed to trigger action.** Copy button, friend-send option, nudge copy — all designed to close the gap between "I should post" and actually posting.
4. **It validates the fear.** "Your story is worth telling" from an AI that just analyzed it hits different than a friend saying "you should totally post that."

---

## Target Users

- **Career returners** — "will people judge my gap?"
- **Career switchers** — "I'm not an expert yet, who wants my take?"
- **First-time founders** — "what if I share my idea and it's dumb?"
- **New grads** — "I haven't done anything worth posting"
- **99% of LinkedIn** — the lurkers who consume but never create

## Distribution

**LinkedIn DM template:**
"I typed in something I've been too scared to post about. This tool showed me why people would actually care. Honestly it called me out hard. Try it: [link]"

**LinkedIn post template:**
"I've been on LinkedIn for 5 years and never posted. I typed my story into this tool. It roasted me for sitting on it this long. Here's the post it helped me unblock: [the actual post]"

**Why it spreads:**
- The roast card is screenshot-worthy
- The meta-story ("tool helped me finally post") is itself a great LinkedIn post
- Every user who actually posts becomes proof that it works
- Targets the 99% of LinkedIn that never posts — massive underserved group
